RetroRenew: history/fun fact

Everything that we know is self taught and learned right from our own expierence. we struggled so you dont have to. Half the time when we service a device, the first step is q-tips, and 95% rubbing alcohol. often times the batteries being left in over the decades is what leads to the death of the overall circut boards.

originally created in 2021, the first product serviced was a 1997 gameboy color lime green, found in a savers store from Providence Rhode Island. Through word of mouth we have been able to assist in repairing and renewing several other gameboy color models, as well as advance, and advance sp models. Photos to come soon under our gallery. Nintendo created about 100 million gameboy units worldwide in its release dating back to 1989

What we do:

Repairs for Vintage and New Handheld Gaming Systems

With the age of the products growing older and odler in tandem with the generation that used them, the need for repairs has increased and sourcing of components has skyrocketed

We seek to provide Customers with an easy repair/procurment process. In addition to repairs we can perform upgrades to displays, hardware, etc. We also aim to build a community that likes to preserve retro handheld or current gaming devices

Revival/Re-sale

With our trade offer section, you can submit your broken, or dusty devices to our location for use in future projects, or for re-sale to aid in other enthusaist repairs.

With that being said we also encourage customers to share their gaming devices they have repaired, or just want to share with the shop community.
